## Project Overview

Customer acquisition is expensive. Sustainable growth comes from understanding existing customers, increasing retention, and maximizing customer lifetime value.

This project analyzes an e-commerce marketplace dataset containing nearly **100,000 orders** to identify key business opportunities related to:

- Customer Retention
- Revenue Growth
- Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
- Product Performance
- Seller Performance
- Geographic Trends
- Delivery Efficiency

The objective was not only to analyze data but to translate insights into **actionable business recommendations** that can support executive decision-making.


## Business Problem

An e-commerce marketplace wants to understand:

- What drives revenue growth?
- How effectively are customers being retained?
- Which products and categories generate the most value?
- Which regions offer the highest growth opportunities?
- How does delivery performance impact customer experience?
- What strategies can increase profitability and repeat purchases?

This project answers these questions through data-driven analysis and visualization.

## Business Questions Answered

This project was designed to answer critical business questions related to revenue growth, customer retention, and marketplace performance.

### 1. How much revenue is the marketplace generating?

**Answer:**  
The marketplace generated approximately **$19.88 million** in revenue from **96,460 orders** placed by **93,341 unique customers**. Revenue trends showed consistent marketplace activity with periods of higher customer demand throughout the analysis period.

**Business Value:**  
Provides leadership with a clear understanding of overall business performance and supports revenue forecasting and growth planning.

### 2. Which product categories contribute the most revenue?

**Answer:**  
The highest revenue-generating categories were **Bed Bath & Table ($1.72M)**, **Health & Beauty ($1.63M)**, **Computers & Accessories ($1.56M)**, **Furniture & Decor ($1.41M)**, and **Watches & Gifts ($1.39M)**. Revenue was concentrated among a relatively small number of categories.

**Business Value:**  
Helps prioritize inventory management, marketing investments, and category expansion strategies.

### 3. Who are the most valuable customers?

**Answer:**  
Customer Lifetime Value analysis revealed that a small segment of customers contributed a disproportionately large share of marketplace revenue. These high-value customers represent the most profitable customer segment for the business.

**Business Value:**  
Supports targeted retention campaigns, personalized marketing efforts, and customer loyalty initiatives.

### 4. How effectively is the marketplace retaining customers?

**Answer:**  
The repeat purchase rate was approximately **3%**, indicating that most customers made only a single purchase. This suggests that customer acquisition is strong, but customer retention presents a significant opportunity for improvement.

**Business Value:**  
Highlights the need for loyalty programs, personalized offers, and post-purchase engagement strategies to increase repeat purchases.

### 5. Which sellers drive the marketplace's success?

**Answer:**  
Seller performance analysis showed that sellers located in **São Paulo (SP)** generated the highest share of marketplace revenue, significantly outperforming other regions. Revenue contribution was concentrated among a limited number of high-performing sellers.

**Business Value:**  
Supports strategic seller partnerships and helps identify best-performing sellers that drive marketplace growth.

### 6. Which regions generate the highest business value?

**Answer:**  
**São Paulo (SP)** generated the highest customer revenue at approximately **$7.45 million**, followed by **Rio de Janeiro (RJ)** and **Minas Gerais (MG)**. These regions represent the marketplace's strongest customer bases and revenue contributors.

**Business Value:**  
Supports regional marketing strategies, expansion planning, and resource allocation decisions.

### 7. How efficient is the delivery process?

**Answer:**  
Delivery analysis indicated that fulfillment performance plays a critical role in customer satisfaction. Timely deliveries contribute to a better customer experience and increase the likelihood of future purchases.

**Business Value:**  
Highlights opportunities to improve logistics efficiency, reduce delays, and strengthen customer trust.

### 8. What factors can drive future revenue growth?

**Answer:**  
The analysis identified four major growth drivers: improving customer retention, increasing repeat purchases, investing in top-performing product categories, and strengthening relationships with high-performing sellers.

**Business Value:**  
Provides a data-driven roadmap for sustainable revenue growth and long-term marketplace success.















## Key Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following strategic actions that I recommended:

- Improve customer retention through loyalty and engagement programs.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing product categories.
- Strengthen partnerships with top-performing sellers.
- Reduce delivery delays to improve customer satisfaction.
- Target high-value customer segments with personalized campaigns.
- Expand operations in regions demonstrating strong demand.
